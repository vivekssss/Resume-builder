# Resume Templates Guide

## Available Templates (6 Professional Styles)

### 1. **Modern Professional**
- **Best For**: General applications, ATS systems
- **Style**: Clean, minimal, blue accents
- **Sections**: Clear hierarchy with bold headers
- **Companies**: Suitable for any industry

### 2. **Amazon/FAANG**
- **Best For**: Top tech companies (Amazon, Meta, Netflix)
- **Style**: Impact-focused, leadership principles aligned
- **Format**: Bullet points with metrics and achievements
- **Key Features**: 
  - Emphasis on quantifiable results
  - Action-oriented language
  - Leadership principles showcase

### 3. **Google Style**
- **Best For**: Google, tech startups, creative tech roles
- **Style**: Colorful accent dots, modern layout
- **Format**: Clean with visual hierarchy using colors
- **Key Features**:
  - Blue, red, green, yellow accent colors
  - Emphasis on projects and impact
  - Modern, friendly design

### 4. **Microsoft**
- **Best For**: Microsoft, enterprise tech companies
- **Style**: Professional blue theme, corporate
- **Format**: Clear sections with emphasis on competencies
- **Key Features**:
  - Blue header and section dividers
  - Technical skills highlighted upfront
  - Grid-based skill layout

### 5. **Apple Minimal**
- **Best For**: Design roles, Apple, minimalist companies
- **Style**: Ultra-clean, centered layout, lots of whitespace
- **Format**: Elegant typography with light fonts
- **Key Features**:
  - Minimalist aesthetic
  - Center-aligned header
  - Focus on content over decoration

### 6. **Creative Modern**
- **Best For**: Startups, creative agencies, design roles
- **Style**: Gradient header, colorful accents
- **Format**: Visual tags, modern card-based layout
- **Key Features**:
  - Purple-blue gradient header
  - Colored section indicators
  - Technology badges/pills

## How to Switch Templates

1. Click on any template in the "Choose Template" section
2. Your resume content automatically adapts to the new format
3. All your information is preserved
4. Preview updates in real-time

## ATS Compatibility

All templates are designed to be ATS-friendly:
- ✅ No tables or complex layouts
- ✅ Standard fonts
- ✅ Clear section headers
- ✅ Proper heading hierarchy
- ✅ Simple formatting
- ✅ PDF export maintains structure

## Best Practices by Template

### For Amazon/FAANG Template
- Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Include metrics (%, $, #)
- Start bullets with action verbs
- Show leadership and impact

### For Google Template
- Highlight innovative projects
- Show cross-functional collaboration
- Emphasize technical breadth
- Include open-source contributions

### For Microsoft Template
- Feature relevant certifications
- Show product management skills
- Highlight stakeholder management
- Include agile/scrum experience

### For Apple Template
- Keep it simple and elegant
- Focus on design thinking
- Show attention to detail
- Highlight user-centric achievements

## Template Selection Tips

1. **Match the company culture**: Use Google template for Google, etc.
2. **Consider your industry**: Tech vs. Finance vs. Creative
3. **ATS first**: If unsure, use Modern Professional
4. **Stand out appropriately**: Creative for startups, minimal for corporations
5. **Test multiple**: Download PDFs of 2-3 templates and compare

## Customization

Each template can be customized further by:
- Editing the template component files in `/components/templates/`
- Adjusting colors, fonts, spacing
- Adding or removing sections
- Modifying the layout structure
